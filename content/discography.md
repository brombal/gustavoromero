### Mompou: Complete Canciones y Danzas / Complete Impresiones Intimas
- **Composer:** Frederic Mompou
- **Performer:** Gustavo Romero (piano)
- **Contents:**
  - Canciones y Danzas Nos. 1-12 (twelve pieces)
  - Impresiones Intimas (nine pieces including "Pajaro Triste," "La Barco," "Cuna," "Secreto," and "Gitano")

### Chopin: Complete Impromptus
- **Composer:** Frederic Chopin
- **Performer:** Gustavo Romero (piano)
- **Contents:** Ten works including Impromptus Nos. 1-4, Fantasie-Impromptu, Barcarolle, Scherzo No. 2, two Polonaises, and two Etudes
- **Purchase:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Chopin-Complete-Impromptus-Other-Works/dp/B000001SI8/ref=sr_1_2?s=music&ie=UTF8&qid=1530554215&sr=1-2&keywords=gustavo+romero)
- **Cover Image:** chopin.jpg

### Beethoven: Five Piano Concertos
- **Composer:** Ludwig van Beethoven
- **Performers:** Gustavo Romero (piano), English Chamber Orchestra, conducted by James Sedares
- **Contents:** Piano Concertos Nos. 1-5:
  - No. 1 in C major, Op. 15
  - No. 2 in B-flat major, Op. 19
  - No. 3 in C minor, Op. 37
  - No. 4 in G major, Op. 58
  - No. 5 in E-flat major, Op. 73 ("Emperor")
- **Purchase:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Piano-Concertos-Ludwig-van-Beethoven/dp/B000001SIO/ref=sr_1_4?s=music&ie=UTF8&qid=1530554215&sr=1-4&keywords=gustavo+romero)
