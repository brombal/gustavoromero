## Press Reviews & Testimonials

### The Washington Post
"A pianist of genius"

### The New York Times
"Mr. Romero has great agility, adequate power and an unusually large repertory of colors and dynamic levels."

### Los Angeles Times (Beethoven)
"The playing is authoritative, incontrovertibly Beethovenian in recreating the composer's style, technically immaculate and compelling."

### Los Angeles Times (Chopin)
"Romero is a Chopin natural, a serious young pianist with singing tone."

### Die Burger, Cape Town South Africa
"...Friday evening Romero made the piano alive: it sang beautifully, then whispered very softly or thundered tremendously."

### San Diego Union-Tribune
"With fleet-fingered dexterity and a strong lyrical impulse, the pianist from Chula Vista adjusted his virtuosity to the musical requirements."

### Sarasota Herald-Tribune, Florida
"This pianist phenomenon radiates an aura of warmth and candor, which endears him to his audience almost before he touches a piano key."

### The Orange County Register, California
"Romero's playing combined a basic patience with a certain tautness of phrase that clarified rhythms, phrases and paragraphs."

### Kenneth Herman, sandiego.com
"It was impossible not to take note of Romero's athletic approach to Haydn's C Minor Sonata."

### Kenneth Herman, sandiego.com (Haydn Variations)
"His Haydn Variations in F Minor, Hob. xvii:6, proved a model of fastidious architecture."

### Ken Herman, San Diego Story
"A master of detail and an elegant sculptor of phrasing..."

### Albuquerque Journal, New Mexico
"Like a kind of philosopher-poet, Romero kept turning the notes over, looking for and finding surprising layers of meaning."

### The Dominion, Wellington, New Zealand
"His articulation must be heard to be believed, and it could only be achieved with the kind of unfettered facility which Romero enjoys."

### The Honolulu Advertiser, Hawaii
